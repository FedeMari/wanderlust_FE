Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which Wanderlust collects, uses, maintains, and discloses information collected from users of the Wanderlust website.

1. Information We Collect

When you visit our website, we may collect personal identification information such as your name and email address. We may also collect non-personal identification information about your visit, such as your IP address and web browser details.

2. How We Use Collected Information

The information we collect from you may be used in the following ways:

  • To personalize your experience on our website.
  • To improve our website and customer service.
  • To send periodic emails with updates and promotional offers.

3. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others. We may share generic aggregated demographic information not linked to any personal identification information regarding visitors and users with our business partners, trusted affiliates, and advertisers.

4. Data Security

We implement security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information stored on our website.

5. Changes to this Privacy Policy

Wanderlust has the discretion to update this Privacy Policy at any time. We encourage users to check this page regularly for any changes.
